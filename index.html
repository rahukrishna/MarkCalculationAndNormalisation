How to Use

  • PT1 (0-40): Periodic Test 1 marks
  • PT2 (0-80): Periodic Test 2 marks
  • PT3 (0-80): Periodic Test 3 marks
  • /5: Best 2 PT scores averaged & scaled to 5
  • Annual (0-80): Annual exam marks
  • SEA, PF (Multiple Assessment), MA (formerly Portfolio) (0-5): Co-curricular activities
  • Final: (/5) + Annual + SEA + Multiple Assessment + MA (shown only when all marks entered)
  • Grade: Based on final marks with grade points
  • Rank: Candidate's position by final marks
  • GPA: Average of grade points across all subjects
